About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer who loves building systems and actual, hands-on tools that solve real-world problems. Over 15 years, I've moved from enterprise SAP rollouts serving millions of users to full-stack builds where I wore every hat — developer, architect, and problem-solver. What drives me isn't the code itself. It's what the code enables: infrastructure that quietly empowers teams, removes friction, and gives companies the foundation to move faster than they thought possible.

Today, that same obsession has pulled me toward AI integration — specifically, the gap between raw data and real intelligence. I'm fond of building LLM Wikis: converting massive, static data repositories into living, conversational knowledge systems that language models can reason with natively. It's not about plugging in an API. It's about rethinking how software thinks — and designing the layer between human intent and machine action.
